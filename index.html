About me

[CV]

I am a research scientist at Adobe Research. I received my Ph.D. degree from University of California San Diego, advised by Prof. Ravi Ramamoorthi. Before that, I obtained Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science with First Class Honors from The University of Hong Kong. I was also once a research assistant at HKU advised by Prof. Yizhou Yu.

My research interests include various topics in computer graphics and computer vision, especially for appearance acquisition and 3D reconstruction.
